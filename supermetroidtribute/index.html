Super Metroid History

Released in 1994, Super Metroid was the 3rd installment in the Metroid game series. It is considered one of the best video games of all time, and was even called the best game of all time in 2003 in Electronic Gaming Monthly. This game is available both on the Wii and the Wii U. More information about the history of Super Metroid can be found at Wikipedia.

Super Metroid Fun Facts

It was the first Metroid game with a Grapple Beam for Samus. Hackers also mined the code for SM to find that it contained several enemies that weren't included in the final version of the game. Additionally, the sounds for the SM bosses were actually sampled from the Godzilla movies. You can read more about interesting Super Metroid facts here.



Super Metroid Hacks

Several hacks of the original Super Metroid have been crafter. The Phazon Hack (first image) features new enemies, rooms, items, etc, and Samus wears a dark colored suit in this hack. Hyper Metroid (second image) features rearranged versions of the original SM levels with a darker spin to the original story line. The Digital Cube hack features a level and map layout that is remenescient of the Cube movies. More information about SM hacks can be found here.
